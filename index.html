Waves Across The Globe

Discover the planet's greatest surf breaks, captured through art, photography, and storytelling.

Waves Across the Globe is an artistic journey into the ocean's most iconic surf breaks. Each location on this map represents more than a pin - it marks a place where water, culture, and human connection meet.

This project brings together art, photography, and storytelling to capture the character of waves around the world. From cold northern swells to warm tropical reef passes, every break has its own rhythm, history, and spirit.

Click on a surf break to explore its story: the visuals, the energy, and the experience that make it unique. Whether you're a surfer, an artist, or simply someone drawn to the sea, this space invites you to travel coastlines, chase swells, and see the world through the language of waves.
